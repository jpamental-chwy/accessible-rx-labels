Instructions

Give 1 capsule by mouth about 3 days weekly. Modified cyclosporine (Atopica, Cyclavance, Sporimmune, generic) can help manage itch and skin inflammation in allergic cats. It is an immunosuppressant that disrupts T cell function, with side effect that include vomiting, diarrhea, viral warts, gum changes, and immunosuppressive effects. Modified cyclosporine requires 3 plus weeks to take effect and is best suited for long-term administration in more severely affected cats. Blood work is recommended for patients receiving long-term modified cyclosporine.
